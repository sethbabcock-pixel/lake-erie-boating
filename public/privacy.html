Privacy Policy

Should I Boat? (shouldiboat.com) · Last updated June 2026

Should I Boat? is a free tool that shows live boating conditions for the Great Lakes. This policy explains what data is involved when you use the site.

Information we collect

We do not require an account and do not ask for your name, email, or location. The app stores small preferences (your selected spot and light/dark theme) in your browser's local storage — this never leaves your device. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies & third-party services

To keep the site free and functional, we use third-party services that may set cookies or collect usage data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — serves ads and may use cookies to personalize them. You can manage ad personalization at Google Ads Settings.
  • Embedded content — the weather map (Windy) and live webcams (Angelcam, Pixelcaster, WTOL, et al.) are embedded from third parties and may set their own cookies.
  • Weather data — conditions come from public NOAA/NWS, NDBC, and Open-Meteo services; no personal data is sent to them.
  • Amazon Associates — some "gear" links are affiliate links.

Affiliate disclosure

As an Amazon Associate, Should I Boat earns from qualifying purchases made through links on this site, at no extra cost to you.

Your choices

You can clear cookies and local storage in your browser at any time. In the EEA/UK, ad and cookie consent is managed through the consent prompt and your browser settings.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@shouldiboat.com.

Conditions are guidance only — always verify against official NWS marine forecasts and use your own judgment on the water.
